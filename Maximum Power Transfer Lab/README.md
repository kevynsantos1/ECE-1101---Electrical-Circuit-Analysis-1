For this lab our goal was to determine what conditions must be in place to see if a load will
produce maximum power. The theory states that to reach maximum load, the load resistance must
equal the internal resistance of the source. As the resistance load increases our load voltage increases
which means the load current will drop. It is important to note that we used 22k resistors instead of 25k,
2.2k resistors instead of 2.7k, and 3.0k resistors instead of 3.3k.
